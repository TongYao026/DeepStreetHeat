Key Findings

  • POI Boost: R² improved 4x (0.03 → 0.17) with activity data.
  • Heterogeneity: Spatial Block CV reveals low generalization (R² < 0).
  • Diagnostics: GWR (R²=0.31) outperforms global model (R²=0.17).

Methodology Details

LST Processing: Annual mean composite of Landsat-8 Collection 2 Level 2 images (2023).

POI Density: Kernel Density / Count per Area in 100m buffer.

GWR Settings: Gaussian GWR with Adaptive Bisquare kernel, bandwidth selected via AICc minimization.
